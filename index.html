National Record Holder · 400m · 45.07s

I am Kalinga Hewa Kumarage, Sri Lanka's national championship record holder in the 400m (45.07s) and a proud representative of my nation on the world athletics stage. My defining career milestone came at the Asian Athletics Championships when I broke an 18-year medal drought for Sri Lanka in the 400m — a moment that proved our nation can compete at the very highest level.

Now, with my sights firmly set on the 2028 Los Angeles Olympic Games, I am seeking a committed partner who shares my vision: to bring a Sri Lankan sprinter to the Olympic podium.

— Kalinga Hewa Kumarage · Asian Championship Medallist
